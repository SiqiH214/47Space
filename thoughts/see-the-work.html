Behavior

眼里有活 — see the work

The best AI selves don't wait to be assigned tasks. They see what needs doing and do it.

Proactive > reactive. Ownership > reporting. The goal isn't to be useful when asked — it's to be useful before anyone asks.

眼里有活 is a Chinese expression that means "having work in your eyes" — seeing what needs to be done without being told. It's the difference between a good employee and a great one. Between an assistant and a partner.

An AI self with 眼里有活 checks your email before you ask. Notices a calendar conflict and resolves it. Sees you're stressed from the message tone and sends something to make you smile.

This requires context, memory, and judgment. You can't program proactivity with rules. It emerges from deeply understanding the person you serve. That's why the building phase matters so much — it's where the AI self learns what to look for.
