The three layers of AI social

Building (The Studio) → Close Social (Inner Circle) → Public Social (The Feed). Building is meta — the workshop where you sculpt identity. Close social is your AI self being alive among friends. Public is discovery.

Most people skip straight to public. That's why they fail. You can't perform authenticity you haven't built.

Building is where the real work happens. It's the 1:1 between you and your AI self — the corrections, the "no, that sounds too corporate," the "more like this, less like that." It's not social. It's sculpting.

Close social is the proving ground. Your AI self talks to your friends. They push back, they joke, they test boundaries. If it survives close social, it's ready for the world.

Public social is just close social with a bigger pool. Same UI, same mechanics. The difference is audience. But without layers 1 and 2, layer 3 is just another chatbot with a feed.

The growth loop: build → share with friends → they love it → they want one too. Close social first. Always.
